Test Recipe

Description

A piece of literal shit is a decandent piece of brown and pink meat with chunks of carrots and onion for flavoring.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 oz pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can canned carrots
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned chicken
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • ½ cup water

Steps

  1. Step 1

    • Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a Dutch oven over medium heat until well browned. Stir in canned carrots and cannced chicken. Simmer, covered, for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  2. Step 2

    • Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Pour ingredients from Step 1 into this pot.

  3. Step 3

    • Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).

  4. Step 4

    • Cook dutch oven for 10 minutes.

  5. Step 5

    • Pour 2 cans of tomato paste over dish before serving.
